# Phase: More (QoL & Advanced Analytics)

This document tracks long-term ideas for Quality of Life (QoL) improvements and advanced study analysis tools.

## 🎵 Music & Atmosphere (Phase 4 Integration)
- [ ] **Song Frequency Analysis**: Track which specific songs are played during Focus sessions.
- [ ] **Genre Distribution**: Visualize focus time broken down by music genre (Lo-fi, Classical, Ambient, etc.).
- [ ] **Artist Spotlight**: Identify which artists help you focus the longest.
- [ ] **Sync with Stats**: Show "Most Listened Song" for a specific day in the Detailed Day View.

## 📊 Advanced Mathematical Tools
- [ ] **Consistency Score**: A mathematical index (0-100) based on daily session variance.
- [ ] **Scatterplot Distribution**: Visualize session start times throughout the day to find your natural "Focus Rhythm."
- [ ] **Focus Histogram**: Distribution of session lengths to see if you prefer 25m, 50m, or custom durations.
- [ ] **Predictive Study Windows**: Use historical data to suggest the "Ideal Period" for your next study session.
